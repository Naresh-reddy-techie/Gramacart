Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

1. Overview

GramaCart is a hyperlocal delivery platform connecting customers with local retailers, sellers, and fulfillment centers.

Due to the nature of on-demand and hyperlocal deliveries, returns and cancellations are subject to the terms below.

2. Order Cancellation

Before Processing

Orders may be cancelled before they are accepted, packed, or prepared by the seller.

After Processing

Once an order has been packed, prepared, assigned for delivery, or dispatched, cancellation requests may not be accepted.

3. Open Box Verification

Where available, customers are encouraged to inspect the delivered products at the time of delivery.

By accepting delivery after inspection, the customer acknowledges that the order has been received in satisfactory condition.

4. Eligible Refund Cases

  • Wrong item delivered.
  • Missing item.
  • Damaged product.
  • Expired product.
  • Order not delivered.
  • Duplicate payment.

5. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Change of mind after delivery.
  • Incorrect product selection by customer.
  • Price changes after purchase.
  • Products consumed or used after delivery.
  • Products accepted during delivery without reporting any issue.

6. Refund Review

GramaCart reserves the right to review and verify refund requests.

Customers may be required to provide photographs, videos, order information, or additional evidence to support claims.

7. Refund Method

Approved refunds may be processed through the original payment method, wallet credit, UPI transfer, replacement item, or other suitable resolution.

8. Fraud Prevention

GramaCart reserves the right to reject refund requests that appear fraudulent, abusive, misleading, or unsupported.

9. Contact Us

For refund or cancellation related queries, please contact GramaCart Support.

Email: contact.gramacart@gmail.com
