Delivery Policy

This Delivery Policy explains how GramaCart processes, manages, and delivers orders placed through our platform.

Effective Date: July 2026

1. Overview

GramaCart is a hyperlocal commerce platform that facilitates the delivery of products from local retailers, sellers, fulfillment centers, and GramaCart-operated facilities within supported service areas.

Delivery availability may vary depending on your location, seller availability, operational conditions, and service coverage.

2. Service Availability

Deliveries are available only in locations where GramaCart currently operates.

Service areas may be expanded, modified, restricted, suspended, or discontinued at any time without prior notice.

3. Delivery Time Estimates

Delivery times displayed on the Platform are estimated and provided solely for customer convenience.

  • Weather conditions
  • Traffic congestion
  • Road closures
  • Seller preparation delays
  • Peak demand periods
  • Technical disruptions

Delivery estimates are not guaranteed delivery commitments.

4. Delivery Charges

Delivery charges may vary depending on:

  • Order value
  • Distance
  • Delivery location
  • Promotional offers
  • Operational requirements

Applicable charges will be displayed before checkout.

5. Customer Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate delivery information.
  • Provide a reachable contact number.
  • Be available during delivery.
  • Respond to delivery-related communication.

6. Failed Deliveries

A delivery may be considered unsuccessful if:

  • Customer is unavailable.
  • Phone number is unreachable.
  • Address details are incorrect.
  • Delivery location is inaccessible.

In such cases, GramaCart reserves the right to cancel the order, charge applicable delivery fees, or deny refund requests where appropriate.

7. Order Verification

Customers are encouraged to verify delivered items at the time of delivery wherever operationally feasible.

Any concerns regarding missing, damaged, or incorrect products should be reported promptly through GramaCart Support.

8. Risk & Ownership

Ownership and responsibility for products transfer to the customer upon successful delivery and acceptance of the order.

9. Force Majeure

GramaCart shall not be liable for delays or delivery failures caused by:

  • Natural disasters
  • Floods or storms
  • Government restrictions
  • Strikes
  • Network outages
  • Public emergencies
  • Transportation disruptions

10. Limitation of Liability

To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, GramaCart's liability relating to delivery services shall be limited to the value of the affected order.
